\# 📱 Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard



\## About the Project



This Power BI dashboard was created to analyze mobile phone sales data and uncover meaningful business insights. The dashboard provides a clear view of sales performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, and product trends through interactive visualizations.



The project focuses on transforming raw sales data into an easy-to-understand dashboard that helps users explore key performance indicators and make data-driven decisions.



\## Objectives



\- Monitor overall sales performance

\- Analyze sales across different cities

\- Identify top-performing mobile brands and models

\- Understand customer ratings and satisfaction

\- Track payment method preferences

\- Visualize sales trends over time



\## Dashboard Features



\### KPI Cards

\- Total Sales

\- Total Quantity Sold

\- Total Transactions

\- Average Sales



\### Interactive Filters

\- Month Filter

\- Mobile Model Filter

\- Payment Method Filter



\### Visualizations

\- Total Sales by City (Map)

\- Total Quantity by Month and Day

\- Total Sales by Mobile Model

\- Transactions by Payment Method

\- Customer Ratings Analysis

\- Total Sales by Day Name

\- Brand-wise Sales and Quantity Summary



\## Key Insights



\- Samsung generated the highest sales among available brands.

\- UPI was the most commonly used payment method.

\- Delhi contributed significantly to overall revenue.

\- Sales performance varied across different days and months.

\- Customer ratings indicated generally positive feedback.
